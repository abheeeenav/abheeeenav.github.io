## Why this PRD?
I could have easily made adsBook, But I thought lets do it in a way like it's done in realtime. So thought of creating a Product Requirements Document, so as to learn to how to make one. Surely things are ambiguous but gives a knack of how PRD looks like. 

## What is adsBook for Indian Marketers?

adsBook is a curated collection of Indian Brands who are killing it in facebook ads space. These swipe files act as ad inspiration for Indian marketers.


## Why build adsBook?



*   Difficult to find good Indian brands for inspiration. Currently the searching process is fragmented. Objective is to create a resourceful repository
*   This makes competition research difficult.
*   Solutions present as of now curate only US based brands.


## Problem it Solves



*   Competitor Research keeping Indian Brands in mind.
*   Ad Inspiration and Ideas
*   Easy to Browse and refer brands from different categories.
*   Reduce research time and curb creative block


## Assumptions Building this Product



*   This eases the research process accompanied while creating ads
*   A substantial amount of good brands are running ads.


## User Stories



*   When strategizing for a new brand, I will look for competitors in my sector and see their messaging and creatives. This helps in dwelling upon what competitors are using. It’s a smart hack to do what competitors are  doing and build upon it.
*   As a marketer, I need to experiment with different angles, and finding inspiration can be difficult. 
*   I am stuck and can’t see anything working, lets go to the drawing board and see what other brands are doing
*   I can’t think of how to design this creative, let’s see how other brands are placing their elements.


## Goal & Vision of this Product



*   Making life of Indian Marketers Easy
*   It particularly helps upcoming marketers and marketers who have just entered the space. This makes strategizing more easy.
*   Saves time while getting Inspiration


## Potential Metrics to watch out for / impact



*   Number of users visiting the site
*   Frequency of visits
*   Number of social shares for impact measuring
